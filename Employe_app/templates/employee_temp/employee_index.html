Description About The System

The Employee Page of the Inventory Management System is where you, as an employee, can log in to make requests for assets and receive notifications regarding your requests. This page serves as a centralized platform for managing asset requests and approvals. Once you log in, you will be directed to a dashboard that displays any pending requests and notifications related to your requests. From here, you can create new asset requests, view the status of previous requests, and track the progress of approved requests. To create a new asset request, simply fill out the required information such as the asset type, quantity, and reason for the request. Once submitted, your request will be reviewed by the authorized personnel who will either approve or reject it based on the availability of the requested asset. If your request is approved, you will receive a notification with details about when and where to collect the asset. In case of rejection, you will also receive a notification with a reason for the rejection. The Employee Page of the Inventory Management System is designed to streamline the asset request process, ensuring that employees have access to the necessary tools and equipment they need to perform their jobs effectively.
